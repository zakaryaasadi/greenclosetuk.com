ABOUT US

Green Closet is a socially responsible company that was founded in 2022 and headerquartered in the United Kingdom. We collect donations of gently used clothing and also purchase pre-owned clothing.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to promote sustainability by collection used clothing and repurposting it. We believe that by giving new life to pre-owned clothing, we can reduce waste and protect the environment as our company covers all regions of the United Kingdom.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to keep as much clothing our of landfills as possible. In addition, Green Closet works closely with communities an organizations to promote environmental awareness and to help those in need. We can make a positive impact on the planet and the people who inhabit it.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to be the leading company in the industry of recycling used clothing. We aim to make an impact in the community by providing jobs and supporting local charities with main goal in mind which is to create a sustainable and better future for the people and our planet.
